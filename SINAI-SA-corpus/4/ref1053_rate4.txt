        Canon SD1100 excellent camera, May 9, 2008
        ByRainer Schulz "photo aficionado" (SFO, CA  USA)  - See all my reviews
The SD1100 is an excellent camera for everyday use.  High quality pictures are easily obtained with image stabilization. Flash is also sufficient, but not great.  The optical viewfinder is not very useful, but when exterior light makes the LCD impossible to see, the viewfinder will do.
