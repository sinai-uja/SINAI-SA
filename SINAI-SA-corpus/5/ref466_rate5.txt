        Nice Camera - Bad Luck, January 15, 2007
        ByRenato R. Souza (Belo Horizonte, MG, Brazil)  - See all my reviews
The Canon Power Shot A630 is a fantastic camera. Easy to use, and excelent shotss are not difficult to get. I had, however, a bad luck with the new feature (the turnable LCD display). It was open while the camera felt down, and it was broken in miliseconds! So be careful while handling it... :-)
