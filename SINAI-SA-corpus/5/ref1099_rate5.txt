        A quality Shirt Pocket Camera point and shoot camera, May 4, 2008
        ByJohn R. Clark  - See all my reviews
I am starting to believe this is the best camera I have ever had.It is very small, but I am beginning to learn how to hangle it.Best lens I have had -- close up -- long distance.  It says 3x zoombut it seems to outperform  my 10x other camera.Image stabilization works.
