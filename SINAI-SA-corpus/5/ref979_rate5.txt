        Canon 1100, January 7, 2009
        ByS. Dickson "aka Grams" (Cincinnati USA)  - See all my reviews
I love this camera.  Even though it is small, just what I wanted, it delivers in almost every way.  It does better in the dark than my CanonA80.  Pictures shot in the dark on the beach aren't perfect, but look great for the absolutely dark situation.
